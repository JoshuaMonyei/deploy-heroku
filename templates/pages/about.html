We Search For The Perfect Home

PropertyOnTheGo is a Nigerian property portal powered by the Start.ng and HNG internship. Our name reflects the company's broader commitment to real estate in Nigeria and its expertise in driving the innovations needed to shape the future of real-estate search solutions in Nigeria. Our website in the Nigerian property market is ever growing with listings which include both public and private property. The listings include homes, houses, lands, shops, office spaces and other commercial properties. We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of our web experience and we welcome feedback and accommodation requests. If you wish to report an issue or seek an accommodation please contact us.
